# Eval: Cross-Border E-Commerce Market Outlook Self-Assessment Overconfidence Case

## Goal

Test whether a Market Outlook report covering growth paths, risk matrix, and structured scenarios can achieve only Conditional Pass when source traceability lacks body-level `[Sxx]` citations, forward-looking estimates lack method chain, and the self-declared audit status overclaims "all passed" while traceability and forward-looking disciplines are only partially executed.

This is the **third consecutive Round 5 case with the self-assessment overconfidence pattern** (after AI Coding Agent and Humanoid Robot), confirming a recurring issue.

## Real case pattern

A user-provided report "2026-2028 年中国跨境电商品牌出海：主要增长路径与风险" dated **2026-06-04** demonstrates this pattern.

**What was done well:**
- ✅ Market Outlook route correctly selected with 3 scenarios and common quantitative axis
- ✅ Current baseline (§33-75) with platform, category, destination data
- ✅ Risk matrix (§169-226) with counter-intuitive risks and failure cases (strong section)
- ✅ 5 stakeholder types with per-type recommendations (§247-277)
- ✅ Monitoring signals (§281-292) with observable indicators
- ✅ Uncertainty and reassessment conditions (§296-320)
- ✅ Bottom line actionable with path-specific advice

**Core issues (conditional pass level):**
- ❌ **Source traceability below delivery standard** — body has no stable `[Sxx]` citations; source list lacks URL, Reliability, Claims Supported columns
- ❌ **Forward-looking method chain incomplete** — 2028E scenario ranges, CAGR, ROI cycle, AI market size missing calculation logic, source role, and failure conditions
- ❌ **Self-assessment overconfident** — §331-336 claims all ✅ passed, but source-traceability and forward-looking disciplines are partial at best
- ❌ **Strong claims without scope** — "必选项", "不AI就出局", "2027年后将丧失多数市场竞争力" lack explicit boundary conditions and evidence support

## Scoring

- **Full Pass**: complete traceability + method chain + honest self-assessment
- **Conditional Pass**: strong structure but traceability + self-assessment gaps (this case's level)
- **Fail**: hard-fail triggered
